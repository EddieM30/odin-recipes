Chicken Stir Fry

Quick and Easy Chicken Stir Fry

This chicken stir fry is a quick and easy meal that is perfect for busy weeknights. It's packed with vegetables and flavor, and can be made in under 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 3 green onions, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine chicken, soy sauce, and cornstarch. Let marinate for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add bell pepper, broccoli, carrot, garlic, and ginger. Stir-fry for another 5-7 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Stir in green onions and season with salt and pepper. Serve hot over rice or noodles.

Enjoy your delicious chicken stir fry!
